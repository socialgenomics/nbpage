Redefining Genomics Data Sharing

Purpose

Our mission is to facilitate efficient and ethical access and use of genomic data for research and diagnostics through the development of tools that enable and facilitate responsible data access and collaboration.

Our Values

  1. We aim to facilitate and promote good data custodianship and ethical data sharing to maximise the benefit for the patient community.
  2. We support accessible, reproducible and citable research data and results.
  3. We respect the individual right to privacy and advocate full adherence to patient consent and data privacy laws at all times.
  4. We actively engage in collaborations with stakeholders in both academia and industry as well as with the patients who consent the usage of their data for research.
  5. We strive to develop Open Source tools for the research community whenever technically and practically possible
