Purpose

Our mission is to facilitate efficient and ethical access and use of genomic data for research and diagnostics through the development of tools that enable and facilitate responsible data access and collaboration.

Our Values

Platform

Our data discovery platform will help you find the datasets and repositories that hold the data that suit your needs. Collaborate with colleagues around the world to speed up and improve the quality of data available for your research.

Our Values

We aim to facilitate ethical data sharing to maximise the benefit for the patient community.

We support accessible, reproducible and citable research data and results.

We respect privacy and advocate full adherence to patient consent and data privacy laws at all times.

We collaborate with academia and industry as well as with patients who consent the usage of their data for research.

We develop Open Source tools for the research community whenever technically possible.
